Empowering Individuals

By leveraging the Retina Framework, Bountium enables people to issue jobs on any matter that can be objectively measured. It could be delivery of a physical item, completion of a digital task, or construction of a publicly funded project - all it takes is the right Oracle, and Bountium can make it happen.

We've provided three Oracles out of the box, just for you:
Blind Trust Oracle

This Oracle trusts the first result sent to it, and pays it accordingly. Its great for a simple task, or an opinion based task with no wrong answer.

Manual Approve Oracle

This Oracle receives a response, and presents it to you, the job poster, for approval. It only pays out once you approve a response. This Oracle is great for when you need 100% satisfaction and control of the end result.

Crowd Verification Oracle

This Oracle provides decentralised approval of your task's completion. A worker submits their response, and a crowd of users is incentivised to assess the quality of the worker's response. This is a great balance between the first two oracles - you get high-quality, and yet hands-free, completion.


In addition to these options, we are standing by ready to design an Oracle for your exact needs - Please contact us to find out how!

We built this solution as an Ethereum decentralised application, which means you can use it anywhere Ethereum is accessible - anonymously!

About Us

Bountium is a project being developed by Sublimity, a blockchain development firm which passionately fights for empowerment of the individual. We believe that giving uncensorable access to work to the entire world will bring opportunity to those who need it most, will drive the world towards meritocracy, and will vastly increase efficiency as work becomes objective, and therefore, goal-driven.
