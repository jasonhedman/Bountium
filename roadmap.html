Roadmap

Bountium is moving forward by leaps and bounds - here's where we've been, and where we're headed!

July 2018

On July 4th, we released our beta and quickly gathered feedback on what workers and job posters thought of Bountium. We are currently working through this advice to ensure Bountium is the best place to get things done online!

This month, we will also engage in community building among both workers and Retina developers. We hope to build an environment of innovation in which developers will deliver perfect Oracles for a variety of Bountium use cases.

August 2018

As we continue to work through feedback, we will begin establishing partnerships with potential users of Bountium. We are actively seeking partnerships with institutions that plan on leveraging our platform, as their organisational structure allows for an efficient design iteration process. Please get in touch if you can help!

In addition to developing partnerships, we will begin testing profile and payout strategies, tailouring our product to help workers get paid in a way that works for them.

Fall 2018

With feedback in hand, we'll be ready to release the main launch of Bountium. We are planning to have a fully developed, beautiful front end, complete with a feed of tasks for workers to choose from, an interface for job posters to pick out their perfect oracle, and a profile page where both parties can see statistics and results for previously posted tasks.

In addition, we will have multiple products built on top of Bountium that will revolutionise their respective industries - watch our mailing list for more word on this!

End of Year 2018

By the end of the year, we will hope to have finalised all features necessary for Bountium, and will be focusing on expanding Bountium's reach and impact.

We will also be exploring new ways to use Bountium, including the viability of a mobile app that can bring labour opportunity to the unbanked of the world, many of whom can only access the web through their phone.
